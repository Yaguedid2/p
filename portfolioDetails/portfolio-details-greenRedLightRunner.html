Project information

  • Name:Red Green Light Runner
  • Project date:March, 2022

Description

In this game, players take on the role of the leader of a group of people navigating through a dangerous and challenging environment, where they have to move without being detected by a powerful enemy, the doll. The main objective of the game is to guide your group to safety while collecting money along the way. This money can be used to purchase you time (more movement without being inspected) As the leader, you are responsible for the movement and safety of your group. The game features beautiful 2D graphics and challenging gameplay that will keep players engaged for hours. With multiple levels, each with increasing difficulty, players will have to use all of their skills to guide their group to safety and collect as much money as possible, without being detected by the doll.
